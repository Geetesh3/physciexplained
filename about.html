About Us


Welcome to PhySci Explained! We are passionate about making physics engaging, understandable, and accessible for students of classes XI and XII. Our focus is on delivering high-quality education tailored to the CHSC (Council of Higher Secondary Education, Odisha) and CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curricula.


At PhySci Explained, we understand that physics can be challenging, but with the right guidance, it can also be incredibly rewarding. Our experienced educators are dedicated to breaking down complex concepts into clear, manageable lessons that help students grasp the fundamentals and excel in their studies.


We believe in a teaching approach that combines clarity, enthusiasm, and practical applications. Our lessons are designed to not only prepare students for exams but also to spark a genuine interest in the subject. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge and skills they need to tackle any problem with confidence.


Join us at PhySci Explained and discover how enjoyable learning physics can be. Together, we'll explore the wonders of physics and help you achieve your academic goals.
